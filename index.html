About Vikas Educational Trust and Vikas Central School

Vikas Educational Trust was founded in 2007-2008 by visionary educationists to preserve our rich educational heritage while evolving for today’s learners. This vision gave rise to Vikas Central School, offering State syllabus education focused on academic excellence and holistic development.
From just 60 students to now over 1,000, the school celebrated its 17th Anniversary in 2024, marking nearly two decades of shaping young minds.
Vikas Early Learning Academy builds strong foundations through early literacy, numeracy, and social-emotional development, fostering confidence and character in every child.
With a practical, engaging curriculum, Vikas prepares students to thrive academically and contribute meaningfully to society.

Proud Recipient of the FAP State Award – Karnataka

We are thrilled to announce that our school has been honored with the esteemed FAP State Award, presented by the Federation of Private Schools and Educational Institutions of Karnataka. This award recognizes outstanding contributions in the field of education within the state.

This moment of pride belongs to our entire school community. We remain dedicated to providing holistic, high-quality education and continuing our journey of excellence in academics, sports, and cultural development.
