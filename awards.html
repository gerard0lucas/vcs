We were awarded 100% results in SSLC in the academic year 2021-2022 from the government.

Vikas Central School takes immense pride in announcing that we were honored by the government for achieving 100% results in the SSLC examinations for the academic year 2021–2022. This remarkable milestone reflects the relentless efforts of our dedicated faculty, the unwavering determination of our students, and the continuous support from our parents and management. Such an achievement not only highlights our commitment to academic excellence but also reinforces our mission to nurture and empower every student to reach their full potential. We look forward to continuing this legacy of success in the years to come.

This outstanding achievement showcases the diverse talents of our students in cultural and creative activities. Their passion, dedication, and enthusiasm, guided by the tireless efforts of our mentors, have brought great honor to our school. Proud Moment at Vikas Central School!

We are delighted to share that our students Kumar Prajwal Y (Class 10) and Deepak S (Class 8) have been honored with the INSPIRE Awards - MANAK 2023-24, a prestigious initiative by the Department of Science & Technology, Government of India.
Each student received a grant of ₹10,000 to develop innovative science projects and will be participating in the District Level Exhibition & Project Competitions (DLEPCs).
This recognition celebrates their creativity, scientific thinking, and dedication to solving real-world problems through innovation. We wish them continued success in their journey of exploration and invention!
