Academics at vikas central school

Our academic team consists of accomplished scholars and passionate educators who drive excellence in teaching, curriculum development, and research. They are dedicated to fostering a dynamic and intellectually stimulating environment for students to thrive and succeed.

Nursery

Nursery education in India is provided to children between 3–6 years by Kindergarten, Playway or Play Schools. Our schools have varying terminology for different levels of classes, beginning from – Nursery,LKG (Lower Kindergarten) and UKG (Upper Kindergarten).

• We help children achieve early years milestones and support their physical development.
• We give children a chance to interact and socialise with peers, nurturing their emotional development.
• We ensure children learn to take care of themselves as part of personal development and understand the natural world.
• Field trips are organized to improve children's social and interactive skills.
• We follow an activity-based learning method where the process of learning happens through performing tasks or activities.

Primary

Primary school education offers numerous benefits that play an important role in a child's development:

• Foundational Knowledge: It lays the groundwork for fundamental subjects like mathematics, language arts, science, and social studies, providing children with essential knowledge.
• Social Development: Children learn to interact with peers, develop friendships, collaborate, and engage in teamwork, focusing on social skills crucial for life.
• Critical Thinking: Education at this level encourages curiosity, problem-solving, and reasoning abilities, nurturing critical thinking skills essential for lifelong learning.
• Basic Life Skills: Primary education introduces basic life skills such as reading, writing, and basic arithmetic, empowering children to navigate everyday challenges.
• Personal Development: It supports emotional and personal growth, helping children understand their strengths, weaknesses, and emotions, thus enhancing self-awareness.
• Routine and Discipline: It establishes routines and instills discipline, teaching the importance of structure, time management, and adherence to rules.

High School

High school is a time for teens to learn valuable skills for life from peers and professionals. School days fill time with fun, informational activities and prepare teens for the world of work. There are many reasons a high school education is important, but most of all it is a means to achieve long-term goals and feel a sense of pride in accomplishment.life skills are learned throughout school-related activities and include:

• Ability to apply knowledge to real-life situations
• Independent thinking skills
• Ability to express thoughts and opinions
• Formation and follow-through on long-term and short-term goals
• Learning to balance duties and responsibilities
• Ability to work with others
