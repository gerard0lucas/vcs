About Vikas Central School

Vikas Educational Trust was founded in 2007-2008 by visionary educationists to preserve our rich educational heritage while evolving for today’s learners. This vision gave rise to Vikas Central School, offering State syllabus education focused on academic excellence and holistic development.
From just 60 students to now over 1,000, the school celebrated its 17th Anniversary in 2024, marking nearly two decades of shaping young minds.
Vikas Early Learning Academy builds strong foundations through early literacy, numeracy, and social-emotional development, fostering confidence and character in every child.
With a practical, engaging curriculum, Vikas prepares students to thrive academically and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

We envision graduates who are compassionate leaders, critical thinkers, and contributors to positive global change, armed with the skills and values to excel in every endeavor they pursue.

Our Mission

Empowering minds, Fostering creativity, Cultivating character; Vikas Central School is dedicated to providing a nurturing environment for students to thrive academically, socially, emotionally and technologically. With a commitment to excellence, discipline, diversity and lifelong learning. At VCS, we aim to inspire the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Management

Our dedicated management team comprises experienced educationists and visionary leaders committed to academic excellence, student well-being, and continuous innovation in learning.
