AWARDS & RECOGNITIONS

Proud Moments at Vikas Central School

Our students and faculty have consistently excelled in various fields, bringing honor to our institution. Here are some of our notable achievements.

ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE

100% Results in SSLC 2021-2022

Vikas Central School takes immense pride in announcing that we were honored by the government for achieving 100% results in the SSLC examinations for the academic year 2021–2022. This remarkable milestone reflects the relentless efforts of our dedicated faculty, the unwavering determination of our students, and the continuous support from our parents and management.

Academic Year 2021-2022
CULTURAL ACHIEVEMENT

21 Medals in Pratibha Karanji Competition

Vikas Central School proudly celebrates a vibrant milestone in co-curricular excellence, having won an impressive 21 medals in the Cluster-Level Pratibha Karanji Competition organized by the Government of Karnataka. This outstanding achievement showcases the diverse talents of our students in cultural and creative activities.

Cluster-Level Competition 2022
SPORTS ACHIEVEMENT

Cluster-Level Kabaddi Champions

On August 23rd, our students demonstrated exceptional skill and teamwork by winning the cluster-level Kabaddi competition. This remarkable achievement qualified them for the taluk-level tournament, showcasing their hard work, determination, and sportsmanship. We are proud of their dedication and look forward to their continued success.

23 August 2022
ATHLETICS ACHIEVEMENT

200m & 400m Sprint Champions

Two of our talented athletes secured third place in both the 200-meter and 400-meter sprint events at the cluster-level competition. Their remarkable speed, endurance, and competitive spirit brought honor to our school and demonstrated the effectiveness of our sports training program.

Cluster Athletics Meet 2022
MARTIAL ARTS

Bengaluru Open Karate Championship

Our students made us proud by winning trophies and medals at the Bengaluru Open Karate Championship. Their outstanding performance and fighting spirit stood out among tough competition.

2022 Karate Championship
DANCE COMPETITION

Runner-Up in Dance Competition

Our students showcased their talent and creativity by securing the runner-up position in a dance competition. Their graceful performances, dedication, and teamwork captivated the audience and judges alike.

2022 Cultural Fest
HANDWRITING COMPETITION

Apsara Handwriting Competition Winners

Our children were delighted to receive goodies and gifts from Apsara Company for their outstanding performance in a handwriting competition. This recognition celebrates their neatness, consistency, and dedication to excellence in written communication.

2022 Handwriting Competition
SCIENCE INNOVATION

INSPIRE Awards - MANAK 2023-24

Our students Kumar Prajwal Y (Class 10) and Deepak S (Class 8) have been honored with the INSPIRE Awards - MANAK 2023-24, a prestigious initiative by the Department of Science & Technology, Government of India. Each received ₹10,000 to develop innovative science projects.
